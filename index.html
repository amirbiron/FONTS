fonts

this is a selection of fonts that i've used over the years. google fonts are included, but most of these are going to be fonts that i've downloaded and use as @font-face. they're all uploaded and ready for you to use if you'd like; justy copy/paste.

the google fonts, which are the first, can easily just be found on google, but they're ones i use regularly; they go in your head tag in your wrappers. the others are all fonts i've collected over the years and will go into your css. you can rename any font where it says font-family: xxx; in the css, you can rename anything. like if you wanted to use brother gothic but didn't want to use the whole name? just change it to brother. but make sure you're using 'brother' as your font-family when you code.

some fonts do show up kind of strange in the header, but they look just fine and normal when you use them.
